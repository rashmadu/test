EDLEE TOURS & TRAVELS is a holiday company in Sri Lanka. We provide Creative, Unique and Pleasurable holiday experiences to suit each traveler’s need.It’s a family based organization as our experience in tourism go way back mid-1970s. I, Asantha Ranasinghe Managing Director of the company proud to continue the journey my Father and mother starts. As directors of our company both of them are still guiding us for perfection.

Promoting less touristic places with hidden attractions is our key elements as it will give more colors to your holiday.
